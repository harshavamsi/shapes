ACM
 



PESITSouth ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) Student Chapter is organization by the students, for the students of PESIT Bangalore South Campus. Having started in 2007, we are in our 10th year. We focus on student development and challenge the field of computing machinery and bring about solutions and change. The chapter helps its members go beyond the books and be part of projects and researches to approach solutions to expected problems by working with teams of like-minded people.

IEEE
 



IEEE is the world's largest professional organization dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. PESIT Bangalore South Campus IEEE Student Chapter is devoted to its aim of instilling scientific temperament in the student community through a plethora of activities around the year. The chapter also introduced a Women in Engineering Affinity Group that is dedicated to promoting women engineers to follow their academic interests to a career in engineering, this group was awarded Most Promising Affinity Group by IEEE Bangalore Section. The chapter is extremely active and has had various events in the past like workshops on Octave & Python, talks on Virtual and Augmented Reality, quizzes, industrial visits to sites like ISRO and HAL, circuit debugging contests, coding and algorithm design competitions, STAR(Student-Teacher and Research Engineer) programs etc. The chapter also encourages and facilitates students to publish IEEE standard papers and attend other IEEE seminars, meetings, webinars and conferences around the country.
